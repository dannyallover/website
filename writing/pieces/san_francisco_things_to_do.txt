Pier 39 - classic place to visit in the city, out of all the piers this one tends to attract the most people; if you go here, definitely get the mini donuts, they’re freshly baked and bomb as fuck, sometimes there’s a line but it’s worth it

Exploratorium - surprisingly fun; all the sights and props are interactive, so unlike a typical museum where you just look at shit, you learn about the pieces by playing with them

Giants game - if there’s a baseball game going on and you’re into that shit, a giants game can be a cool experience; the stadium is beautiful, and you feel like you’re embracing american culture, since baseball played a huge role in building our nations narrative; bring a jacket and/or cuddle buddy it gets cold

Dolores park - one of my favorite places to grab a few beers/bottle of wine and go chill with friends; if you have a friend who plays the guitar or uke, tell them to bring it, or bring a speaker to play some music; people sell tree at Dolores, so if you’re feeling like getting nice and relaxed buy the famous edibles

Marina - the Marina has some dank Mexican food, hard to pin down a particular place, but if you’re looking for some authentic ass Mexican food, you’ll find it here

Twin Peaks - an absolute beauty; if you haven’t been there before it’s a must see; I haven’t seen a view like it that compares; you see the whole bridge as well the city in its entirety; great spot for photos

San Francisco Zoo - one of the best zoos (better than San Diego’s); the zoo is divided up by the animals they inhabit the different parts of the world

IMAX Theatre - SF has a really nice IMAX theatre; if you feel like chilling, kicking back your feet and watching a film, here’s the place to go

Union Square - cool place to kick it in the city, grab a coffee, and chat with some fiends; there’s a mall right next to it and some outdoor stores (some bouge some less)

Ocean Beach - not the prettiest beach, but nevertheless a beach; I liked going here a lot with the boys and throwing a football around or kicking the soccer ball around
